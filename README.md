# IIF Global Macro Views — Europe’s Second China Shock

This repository hosts the research report written during my internship at the Institute of International Finance. The report examines a structural shift in the China–Europe economic relationship, arguing that Europe’s new competitive pressure from China is driven less by the headline trade imbalance and more by technological convergence in high-value manufacturing sectors such as autos, machinery, and clean technology.

- **Author:** Marcello Estevão, Ugras Ulku, Gene Ma, Phoebe Feng, Leila Hamilton, Meshal Alahmadi, Runshi Xue
- **Date:** March 3, 2026
- **File:** [europes-second-china-shock.pdf](./Europe's Second China Shock.pdf)
